Questions
Most and frequently asked questions

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a 5v5 multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Moonton, where players team up and compete in real-time battles using unique heroes with various roles and skills.

You can download Mobile Legends for free from the Google Play Store (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS). Just search “Mobile Legends: Bang Bang” and tap install.

Yes, Mobile Legends is free to play. However, it offers optional in-game purchases like skins, heroes, and battle passes that can enhance your experience.

To play with friends, simply add them using their in-game ID or username. Once added, you can invite them to your squad, team up, and play matches together.

No, Mobile Legends requires a stable internet connection as it is an online multiplayer game. A good connection ensures smooth gameplay and fair matchmaking.

New heroes are usually introduced every few weeks or during major updates. Moonton frequently adds content to keep the game fresh and exciting for all players.
